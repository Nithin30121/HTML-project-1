Interesting Facts About India

1. Zero Was Invented in India

Indian mathematician Aryabhata introduced the concept of zero, revolutionizing mathematics forever.

2. Yoga Originated in India

Yoga is over 5000 years old and originated in ancient India before spreading worldwide.

3. World's Largest Democracy

India has more than 900 million eligible voters, making it the world’s largest democracy.

4. Chess Was Invented in India

The modern game of chess evolved from the ancient Indian game *Chaturanga*.

5. Home to the Oldest City

Varanasi is believed to be one of the oldest living cities in the world, over 3000 years old.

6. Ayurvedic Medicine is 5000+ Years Old

Ayurveda, the world’s oldest healthcare system, began in India and is still widely practiced.

7. India Has 40+ UNESCO Sites

India is home to over 40 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Taj Mahal and Hampi.

8. World's Largest Postal Network

India has more than 1.5 lakh post offices — the largest postal system in the world.

9. India Invented Buttons

Buttons made from seashells were first used in the Indus Valley Civilization around 2000 BCE.

10. India Has the Highest Rail Network

Indian Railways employs over 1.4 million people and operates one of the world’s largest rail networks.
